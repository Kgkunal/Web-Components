• The accordion is a graphical control element comprising a vertically stacked list of items, such as labels or thumbnails. Each item can be "expanded" or "collapsed" to reveal the content associated with that item.
  • The accordion gives users the ability to reveal and hide relevant sections of content. The Accordion component can be found inside a component container, section or panel and can be placed in areas of the system to organize and hide content.
